About Us

We take pride in our eyewear, genuinely handcrafted with unparalleled workmanship.

Our artisanal eyewear journey started in 2002. Inspired by the beauty and excellent functionality of vintage eyeglasses, we design our own collection, source sustainable materials and manufacture one-of-a-kind eyeglasses in our own studio.

Step into a world where time slows, and the art of eyewear takes center stage. Our studio is a haven for genuine craftsmanship, where each pair of eyeglasses is meticulously handcrafted honoring timeless techniques passed down through generations.

We believe in the enduring beauty of handcrafted objects. We also believe that those objects will resonate with like-minded people.

Experience the difference of eyewear crafted with uncompromising attention to detail and genuine passion for eyeglasses.
